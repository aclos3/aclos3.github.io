Add Product Here:

Adding a product will add a product record to the database, and will also add a row to the intersection table "products_sales." The primary key of the product record will be a foreign key in the product_sales table.









Add Sale Here:

Adding a sale will add a sale record to the database, and will also add a row to the intersection table "products_sales." The primary key of the sale record will be a foreign key in the product_sales table.
