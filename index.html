Allow me to introduce myself!

Hello and welcome to my website. My name is Andrew Clos and I live in Newport, OR. I graduated from the University of Washington in 2008 with a BS in Chemical Oceanography. This led to a my first career with the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) where I worked as a contractor and then served as a NOAA Corps Officer until early 2019. In addition to deck watch officer duties (navigating ships, managing personnel), I specialized in the making nautical charts. Utilizing multibeam echosounders and sophisticated positioning systems, we could create high resolution bathymetric plots of the sea floor.

I am looking forward to this career change and would love to learn about A.I. and apply that to autonomous vessels that collect data at sea. However, as I move through the program I will keep a very open mind and may find a completely different passion within the field. In my free time I like to work on classic cars, ski, go camping, play frisbee.
