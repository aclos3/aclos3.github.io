Jenna primarily grew up on the east coast, but always knew she wanted to settle in the Pacific Northwest. When & Where

When:

  • 2:00pm
  • Saturday, August 29th, 2020

Guests may begin arriving at 2pm and can expect drinks and mingling for an hour. At 3pm, the bride and groom will have a brief exchange of vows in front of their family and friends. Feel free to bring your favorite stories of Andrew and Jenna to share! There will be heavy appetizers and cake available to guests throughout the day.

Where:

  • The Clos Residence
  • Gig Harbor, WA

This casual waterside venue is located at the home of Kerrie and Richard Clos-- Andrew's parents. You can expect tents for shade and comfortable shoes are encouraged as there are some sloped areas in the lawn. Parking areas will be designated near the house with assistance provided day-of.
