When & Where

When:

  • 2:00pm
  • Saturday, August 29th, 2020

Guests may begin arriving at 2pm and enjoy drinks and mingling. At 3pm, the bride and groom will have a brief exchange of vows in front of their family and friends. Feel free to bring your favorite stories of Andrew and Jenna to share! There will be heavy appetizers and cake available to guests throughout the day.

Where:

  • The Clos Residence
  • Gig Harbor, WA

This casual waterside venue is located at the home of Kerrie and Richard Clos -- Andrew's parents. You can expect tents for shade and comfortable shoes are encouraged as there are some sloped areas in the lawn. Parking areas will be designated near the house with assistance provided day-of.

Lodging & Attractions

Lodging Options:

With such an intimately sized event, we aren't reserving a block of rooms at a local hotel. Instead, we found a few of our favorites and have shared them in the links below. The Maritime Inn is right in the heart of downtown, across from the city park and boatyard. The Inn at Gig Harbor is very convenient since it is the SEATAC airport shuttle stop for Gig Harbor. The Best Western is just up the hill from downtown and is quite nice. In addition to these lodging options, we recommend checking out vacation rentals on Airbnb and VRBO!

  • Maritime Inn
  • Inn at Gig Harbor
  • Best Western Wesley Inn & Suites
